The Workflow

My approach to managing complexity: A cycle of empathy and system design.

Step 01

Anticipate

Deeply listen to stakeholders to uncover hidden needs and predict potential friction points before they arise.

Step 02

Design

Create workflows that respect human habits. Systems should serve people, not force them to change.

Step 03

Connect

Bridge the gaps between silos (e.g., IT vs. Admin). Translate languages so everyone is on the same page.

Step 04

Empower

Hand over tools and documentation that allow teams to own the system confidently after I leave.
