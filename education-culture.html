The Origin

From Third Culture Kid to Global Educator

My educational philosophy is deeply rooted in my own cross-cultural journey. Growing up in international school environments (GSIS, Tanglin, Taipei British School) gave me a firsthand appreciation for diverse perspectives.

This experience fostered a deep cultural humility—a core counseling trait. It informs my work with minority families and my approach to inclusive education, ensuring that every learner feels seen, valued, and understood regardless of their background.
